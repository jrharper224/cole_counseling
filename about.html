Monica Cole M.S., LPC

Monica Cole is a Licensed Professional Counselor. She has a Bachelor of Science in Interdisciplinary Studies with an emphasis in Special Education from Texas A&M University. Monica taught for four years as a Resource, Special Education teacher. She earned a Master of Science in Counseling and Development from Texas Woman’s University. Monica completed her practicum experience and internship at the Children’s Advocacy Center of Collin County where she worked extensively with children and adolescent survivors of abuse and their non-offending family members. Her post graduate internship was completed at Interfaith Family Services, a non profit organization that strives to “empower families in crisis to break the cycle of poverty”. Monica’s post graduate internship also included her work experience at The Looking Glass Counseling Group, LLC.

Monica’s skills include individual therapy for children, adolescents, and adults, group therapy, couples therapy, play therapy, and family therapy.
